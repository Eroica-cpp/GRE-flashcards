  \item \textbf{vt. 使颜色、音调等柔和: to soften the tone, color, shade, or hue of}
  \begin{itemize}
    \item \em{\Large{mute a color 对颜色进行柔化}}
  \end{itemize}
  \item \textbf{vt. 减弱声音: to soften or muffle the sound of}
\end{enumerate}
